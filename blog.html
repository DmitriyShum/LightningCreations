  • 2/25/21: How I filled a hole in a wooden board with plastic

    • Plastic material is everywhere. Spoons, forks, knives, plates, and more. Articles write that people will be rebuilding/fixing roads with melted plastic in the near future. By melting a spoon, I made liquid plastic that will stick easily to anything. Using basic tools such as a lighter, pliers, sanding paper, I was able to fill a hole in a piece of a wooden board.
